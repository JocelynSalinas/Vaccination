Preparations Before Vaccine

The succes of vaccines is strongly influenced by the streght of the body's immune system. Therefore, there are several things that can be tried to make the COVID-19 vaccine work:

Ingesting large amounts of alcohol, especially over the long term, could contribute to the weakening of the immune system and alter or interfere with the vaccine response against the coronavirus or its different strains. By weakening the immune system with alcohol, it takes time for the human body to recover antibody levels against any type of virus, including SARS-CoV-2.

Stress has a proud effect on inmune work,Moreover, prolonged stress can increase cortisol production and oxidative stress in the body.

At a time when we are facing a pandemic, the concept of strengthening the immune system to protect us from Sars-Cov-2 and other infectious agents has come to the fore. There is talk of consuming vitamins C and D, eating lots of fruit and vegetables and taking supplements such as selenium.

It is just as important to eat well as to sleep well in order to strengthen our immune or defence system; while we sleep, this system is strengthened to protect us against viruses and germs.

Physical activity can help remove bacteria from the lungs and airways. This can reduce the chances of catching a cold, flu or other illnesses. Exercise causes changes in antibodies and white blood cells (WBCs).
